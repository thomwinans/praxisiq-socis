## Issues Found and Fixed

### 1. Flaky Intelligence Integration Tests (HttpClient Timeout)
- **Symptom**: `QuestionIntegrationTests.AnswerQuestion_ValidAnswer_ReturnsAccepted`, `UnlockIntegrationTests.AnswerConfirmRelationship_Yes_CreatesConnectionRecord`, and `UnlockIntegrationTests.AnswerEstimateValue_UpdatesPracticeData` intermittently failed with `TaskCanceledException` due to 15-second HttpClient timeout.
- **Root cause**: Intelligence integration tests hit DynamoDB through Kong. Under parallel test load, DynamoDB Local response times exceed 15 seconds. These are integration tests, not performance benchmarks — the timeout was too aggressive.
- **Fix**: Increased HttpClient timeout from 15s to 30s in all three Intelligence test files:
  - `QuestionIntegrationTests.cs`
  - `IntelligenceIntegrationTests.cs`
  - `UnlockIntegrationTests.cs`
- **Verification**: After fix, both Run 1 and Run 2 pass with 0 failures.
